Overview

These guides cover everything construction professionals need to know about daily reporting, documentation best practices, lien compliance, and field workflows. Construction daily reports are more than paperwork — they are your first line of defense against payment disputes, false claims, and lien filings. A well-documented daily log captures crew hours, materials installed, weather conditions, delays, verbal change orders, and site incidents. When a dispute arises months later, contemporaneous daily reports are the most persuasive evidence available.
