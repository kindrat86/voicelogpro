TL;DR: Learn what makes a construction daily report admissible as evidence in disputes.

The four requirements

Court-admissible daily reports need: (1) contemporaneous timestamps, (2) identifiable author, (3) consistent format, (4) photo evidence.

Contemporaneous records

Reports created at the time of the event carry more legal weight than retroactive documentation.

Photo evidence best practices

Photos should be geotagged and timestamped. Include context shots, not just close-ups.
