How to Dispute Valet Damage Claims

A step-by-step guide to documenting and winning valet damage disputes.

Document the damage immediately

Take photos and videos of the damage as soon as you notice it, before leaving the valet area. Timestamp and location data are critical.

Obtain the pre-handover record

If you have a condition report from drop-off, this is your strongest evidence. CarShake creates these automatically.

Notify the valet manager in writing

File a formal report with the valet company before leaving the premises. Get a copy with a timestamp.

File with your insurance

Contact your auto insurance and provide all documentation. They will pursue the valet's insurance.

Consider small claims court

If the valet denies responsibility and insurance doesn't cover it, small claims court is a cost-effective option with proper documentation.
