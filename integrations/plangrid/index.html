VoiceLogPro + Plangrid: Integration Overview

Integrating VoiceLogPro with Plangrid allows your team to generate daily reports by voice and push them into the project management ecosystem you already use. No double entry, no file format conversions, no manual PDF uploads.

How the Integration Works

VoiceLogPro exports daily report PDFs that can be attached to Plangrid projects, shared with the project team, or stored for compliance purposes. The integration eliminates the gap between field documentation and the office record — ensuring that every daily report captured by voice is available in your central project management system.

Benefits for Your Team

  • Faster daily reports — voice capture in the field, automatic PDF generation
  • Consistent documentation across all projects and trades
  • Court-admissible output that strengthens your position in disputes
  • No manual data entry or file conversion overhead

Practical Application

Every construction project is different, but the fundamentals of good daily reporting are universal: record what happened, when it happened, who was involved, and what changed. VoiceLogPro makes this practical by letting crews speak their report while they walk the site — no typing, no handwriting, no end-of-shift catch-up. The platform handles the structure, timestamps, and PDF formatting so your team can focus on the work.
