VoiceLogPro + QuickBooks Integration: Daily Reports to Job Costing

How VoiceLogPro daily reports feed into QuickBooks job costing. Track labor hours, equipment hours, and materials for accurate per-project profitability.

VoiceLogPro daily reports capture crew hours, equipment hours, and materials at the source. Export this data to QuickBooks for accurate job costing and progress billing.

How It Works

Each VoiceLogPro daily report includes structured fields for labor (by crew member), equipment (by unit), and materials (by type and quantity). This data maps directly to QuickBooks job costing categories.

Job Costing Workflow

  1. Record daily report by voice — include crew names, hours, equipment used, and materials consumed.
  2. Download the PDF report — structured with clear sections for labor, equipment, and materials.
  3. Enter time in QuickBooks — use the daily report's crew hours for timesheet entry against the job code.
  4. Log equipment hours — enter equipment costs in QuickBooks job costing against the project.
  5. Record material usage — match material quantities from the daily report to vendor invoices in QuickBooks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does VoiceLogPro sync directly with QuickBooks?

VoiceLogPro produces structured daily reports that you reference for QuickBooks entry. Direct API integration with QuickBooks Online is planned for Q4 2026.

How do daily reports improve job costing accuracy?

Daily reports capture actual hours and materials at the source, the same day. This eliminates the end-of-week reconstruction problem where contractors estimate hours from memory, typically undercounting by 10-15%.

Can I use VoiceLogPro data for progress billing?

Yes. The daily report's work-performed section documents exactly what was completed, supporting AIA progress billing applications (G702/G703).
