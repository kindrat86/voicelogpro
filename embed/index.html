FAQ

Is it really free? Forever?

Yes. The embedded widget is free forever. No usage limits, no watermarks, no upsells inside the widget. We offer this because it introduces contractors to VoiceLogPro's voice-to-PDF app — but the embedded generator stays free regardless.

Do my users' reports get sent to VoiceLogPro?

No. All report generation happens inside the user's browser. Nothing is transmitted to any server. The data never leaves your visitor's device.

Can I customize the widget's appearance?

The widget adapts to its container width and uses your site's font scaling. For deeper customization (brand colors, logo, white-label), contact us.

Will it slow down my site?

No. The widget loads asynchronously with loading="lazy" — it won't affect your page speed scores. The generator itself is vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with zero external dependencies.
