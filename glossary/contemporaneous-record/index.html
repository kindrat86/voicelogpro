Even experienced crews make mistakes when documenting Contemporaneous Record. The most common errors include: recording incomplete information (missing dates, locations, or crew names), waiting too long to document (relying on end-of-shift recall), and failing to attach supporting evidence like photos or material receipts. Each of these gaps can be exploited in a construction dispute.
