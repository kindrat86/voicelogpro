How the math works

Time to type = Words per day ÷ Typing speed (wpm). Average typing speed is 40 WPM.

Time to speak = Words per day ÷ Talking speed (wpm). Natural conversation is ~150 WPM.

Minutes saved per day = Typing time − Speaking time.

Hours saved per month = (Minutes saved per day × Days per week × 4.33) ÷ 60.

Productivity gain = (Typing time ÷ Speaking time) × 100%. Voice logging is typically 3–5× faster than typing for field workers dictating reports.

💡 Pro tip: Most construction foremen log 300–800 words per daily report. At 150 WPM talking vs 40 WPM typing, that's 5–13 minutes saved per report.
