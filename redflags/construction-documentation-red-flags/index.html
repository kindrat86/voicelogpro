6 Construction Documentation Red Flags

These are the six documentation gaps that lose payment disputes, delay claims, and lien rights. If your daily logs have any of these, they won't hold up when it matters.

1. No timestamps. "Sometime last week" is not evidence. Courts need specific dates. VoiceLogPro timestamps every report to the second.
2. Illegible handwriting. Paper logs written in the truck at end of day. If a judge can't read it, it doesn't exist. Voice-to-text solves this permanently.
3. No photos. "The concrete was poured correctly." Prove it. Every VoiceLogPro report supports photo attachments with metadata.
4. Missing geolocation. "I was on site." Opposing counsel: "Prove it." Auto-geotagging proves on-site presence at the moment of the report.
5. Gaps in the record. 3 weeks of daily logs, then nothing for 2 weeks. Gaps destroy credibility. VoiceLogPro's daily reminder keeps the chain unbroken.
6. No weather documentation. Weather caused the delay but you didn't record it. VoiceLogPro auto-appends weather data to every report.
