Construction Documentation Red Flags

Red flags in construction documentation, subcontractor payments, and field records that signal dispute risk. Know what to fix before a claim becomes a loss.

Overview

Knowing the red flags in construction documentation can save your business thousands in disputed payments, legal fees, and lost lien rights. These resources highlight the most common documentation mistakes that contractors make and show you how to avoid them using VoiceLogPro's voice-to-PDF daily reporting system.

Common red flags include inconsistent daily reports, missing weather data, lack of photo documentation, unverified crew hours, and failure to document verbal change orders. Each of these creates openings for GCs or owners to deny payment, reject change orders, or challenge the scope of completed work. Voice-based daily reporting eliminates many of these risks by making it easy to capture complete, consistent entries every day.
