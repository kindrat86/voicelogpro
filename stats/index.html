Accuracy

95%+
State-of-the-art ASR word error rate
State-of-the-art automatic speech recognition (ASR) systems now achieve word error rates below 5% (95%+ accuracy) for clean English speech, approaching human parity. Domain-specific models (medical, legal) achieve even lower error rates.
Source: Industry benchmarks; Speechmatics, OpenAI Whisper v3
APA: Speechmatics. (2026). Voice AI in 2026.
12–18%
WER on accented or noisy speech
Word error rates rise to 12–18% on heavily accented speech or noisy environments without domain-specific fine-tuning. The gap between clean and noisy speech accuracy is the primary frontier for production voice AI systems.
Source: Industry benchmarks; academic ASR evaluations
APA: VoiceLog Pro. (2026). ASR Accuracy Analysis.
