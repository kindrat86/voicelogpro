Frequently Asked Questions

Is VoiceLogPro really a good Sonix alternative?

It is if your goal is construction daily reporting rather than general transcription. Sonix gives you accurate text from audio; VoiceLogPro gives you a structured daily log — crew, work performed, materials, weather, delays — as a timestamped PDF. For transcribing interviews or meetings, Sonix remains the better fit.

How much does VoiceLogPro cost compared to Sonix?

VoiceLogPro has transparent, public pricing designed to be accessible. Sonix's pricing typically requires a sales call.

Can I import my data from Sonix?

There is no automated importer, and you rarely need one: daily reports start fresh from the day you switch. Export your existing Sonix transcripts and archive them for your records, then use VoiceLogPro for new field logs going forward. The two histories can sit side by side without conflict.
