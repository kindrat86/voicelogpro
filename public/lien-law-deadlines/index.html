Why This Data Matters

Missing a mechanics lien deadline by one day can void your right to payment. A subcontractor who finishes work in Texas has until the 15th day of the 4th month to file — not the 16th. In California, failing to serve a 20-day preliminary notice means you lose lien rights entirely. This reference exists so you never miss a deadline.
