About VoiceLogPro

VoiceLogPro was built by subcontractors who learned the hard way that missing daily reports cost more than bad work.

The Problem We Lived

We spent years on construction sites — conduit in one hand, phone in the other. Every day we'd finish work, look at the daily report form, and think: I'll type it up tonight. Then the truck broke down. Or the kids needed help with homework. Or we were just too exhausted from 10 hours in the Texas heat.

Weeks later, when the general contractor hit us with a $15,000 back charge for "undocumented delays," we had no contemporaneous records. No timestamps. No weather data. No proof of what actually happened on site. The work was done — we just couldn't prove it.

That's when we learned: what kills subcontractors isn't bad work. It's late or missing daily reports.

Why Voice-to-PDF Changes Everything

Typing a daily report after a long day on site is the bottleneck. But talking? You can do that while you're still on site, while the day's events are fresh, with your hands still dirty. VoiceLogPro takes your spoken notes — crew names, work performed, materials installed, delays encountered — and structures them into a timestamped, court-admissible PDF with automatic weather data and photo attachments.

No typing. No "I'll do it tonight." Just speak, and the report is done.

Built for the Trades, by the Trades

VoiceLogPro isn't a general-purpose transcription tool repackaged for construction. Every feature was designed for subcontractors who work with their hands:

  • Offline-first: Works on jobsites with no cell signal. Syncs when you're back online.
  • Trade-specific fields: Electricians get panel schedules and circuit tracking. Plumbers get pressure test records. Roofers get square-footage and fastener-pattern logs.
  • Multi-jurisdiction compliance: Built-in support for Texas Property Code Chapter 53 lien rights, NSW Security of Payment Act adjudications, and UK Building Safety Act Golden Thread requirements.
  • One-button operation: Hit record, speak, done. The PDF is ready in 60 seconds.

What We Believe

Subcontractors do the actual work on every construction project. When payment disputes arise, the party with the best contemporaneous documentation wins — every time. VoiceLogPro exists to make sure that's you.

We believe that documenting your work should be as easy as doing it. That a voice note from the jobsite should be worth more in court than a typed report written from memory three days later. And that every subcontractor — from one-man electrical shops to 50-crew plumbing contractors — deserves tools that protect their right to get paid.
