How to Write a Construction Daily Log

Step-by-step guide to writing effective daily logs that protect your business.

A well-written daily log can be the difference between winning and losing a dispute. Here is how to write logs that hold up legally and provide real value.

Essential Daily Log Elements

  • Date, project name, and weather conditions
  • Workers present (by trade and count)
  • Work performed (by area and trade)
  • Materials delivered
  • Equipment used
  • Delays and their causes
  • Visitors and inspections
  • Safety incidents
