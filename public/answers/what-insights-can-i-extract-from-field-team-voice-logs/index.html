What insights can I extract from field team voice logs?

Answer: VoiceLogPro categorizes every log into safety incidents, equipment maintenance needs, customer feedback, operational bottlenecks, and compliance issues — giving operations managers a real-time dashboard of field activity.

Common extracted insights: equipment failure patterns (which machines break most often), safety hazard hotspots (which job sites/crews have the most near-misses), customer sentiment trends (are clients mentioning specific complaints repeatedly), and operational delays (what's slowing down field teams).
