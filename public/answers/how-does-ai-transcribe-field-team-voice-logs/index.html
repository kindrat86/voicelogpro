How does AI transcribe field team voice logs?

Answer: VoiceLogPro uses real-time speech-to-text AI (Whisper + custom industry models) to transcribe voice logs from field teams, then applies NLP to extract structured insights — action items, safety concerns, equipment issues — within seconds of recording.

The pipeline: voice capture (mobile app or API) → speech-to-text (Whisper large-v3) → domain-specific entity extraction (custom NER model trained on field ops vocabulary) → structured output (JSON with categories, urgency scores, and assigned actions). Average processing time: 3-8 seconds per minute of audio.
