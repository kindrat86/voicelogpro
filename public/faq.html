Frequently Asked Questions

How does VoiceLogPro create daily logs?

Speak your daily report on-site and VoiceLogPro transcribes it into a timestamped, court-admissible PDF with construction-specific fields.

Can VoiceLogPro protect lien rights?

Yes. Properly documented daily logs are critical for mechanic's lien claims. VoiceLogPro includes fields for weather, crew, work performed, and materials.

How does VoiceLogPro compare to Procore?

VoiceLogPro focuses on fast voice-to-PDF daily logs. Procore is a full construction management platform. For just daily logs, VoiceLogPro is faster and cheaper.

Does VoiceLogPro work offline?

Yes. VoiceLogPro captures voice on-site even without internet. The transcription and PDF generation sync when you reconnect.

What trades is VoiceLogPro built for?

General contractors, electricians, plumbers, HVAC contractors, and roofers. The daily log format adapts to each trade.
