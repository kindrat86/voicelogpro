How to write a construction daily log
A construction daily log should include: date, weather, crew on-site, work performed, materials delivered, equipment used, delays, and safety incidents. VoiceLogPro automates this with voice.
Are daily construction logs legally required?
Daily logs are required for mechanic's lien claims in most US states. They document progress, delays, and change orders that protect your payment rights.
How to document change orders
Document change orders with: description of work, reason, cost impact, schedule impact, and approval signatures. VoiceLogPro includes change order tracking fields.
