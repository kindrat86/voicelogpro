Understanding Mechanics Lien Deadlines

A state-by-state overview of mechanics lien filing deadlines and how daily reports support claims.

Why deadlines matter

Missing a lien filing deadline means losing your right to file at all. Deadlines range from 30 to 90 days after last work or material delivery.

How daily reports help

Daily reports prove the date of last work, which starts the clock on your lien deadline. Without them, establishing this date is difficult.

State-by-state variation

Each state has different deadlines, notice requirements, and preliminary notice rules. Always check your specific state's requirements.
